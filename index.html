about me

My name is Olka Dąbrowska and I am an aspiring front-end developer.  more less

After a few years in pubishing I decided to broaden my horizons by learning how to translate traditional typographic rules to modern web communication. To do that I joined front-end development bootcamp.

And it was great. I've never knew, how many new things I would need to learn to achieve my goal: be able to code websites, which are both usefull and appealing for visitors. And now I know.

But also: back then I wasn't able to write websites, such as the one you are just looking at, or those you find below. Feel free to check them out – I hope you'll enjoy it.
